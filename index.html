Marcos Lage

mlage@ic.uff.br

I'm an associate professor at the Instituto de Computação at UFF. I earned my B.Sc. and Ph.D. in Applied Mathematics at PUC-Rio. In 2015 and 2018, I was a viiting scholar at NYU. My research interests are in Data Visualization and Visual Analytics.
